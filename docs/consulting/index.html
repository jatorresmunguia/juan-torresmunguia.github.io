Consulting

I offer a variety of consulting services tailored to research institutions, NGOs, international organizations, and policymakers, aiming to support them in all stages of the data life cycle.

I have provided my services as a consultant to the following organizations:

  • Internal Displacement Monitoring Centre;
  • United Nations Institute for Disarmament Research;
  • International Organization for Migration;
  • UN Women; and,
  • United Nations Office on Drugs and Crime.

Services

Research design

Tasks include:

  • Identifying the target population,
  • Designing indicators,
  • Developing survey questionnaires, and

Data management

Read more +

Tasks include:

  • Identification of data sources;
  • Gathering data from various sources;
  • Cleaning, structuring, and transforming messy data into research-ready datasets; and,
  • Organizing and storing data.

Experience:

I have experience in data cleaning, as well as creating data dictionaries and metadata. Additionally, I have worked on integrating data from household surveys, administrative records, censuses, and remote sensing sources. When statistical information is unavailable, I have leveraged LLMs, web scraping, and text and data mining techniques to generate new datasets.

Example:

I am the author of the global dataset of pandemic- and epidemic-prone disease outbreaks, which earned the 2023 ENLIGHT Open Science Award and was a finalist for the Falling Walls Science Breakthroughs in Science & Innovation Management.

Additionally, I led the data management and analysis of a survey conducted in 2022 across four key locations in Iraq previously occupied by ISIL.

Statistical analysis

Tasks include:

  • Linear regression and nonlinear regression modeling;
  • Machine learning predictive modelling;
  • Disaggregated analyses; and,
  • Statistical tests to assess differences between populations.

Experience:

I have experience analyzing and interpreting data on victimization, gender-based violence, food security, income poverty, displacement, and armed conflict. Additionally, I have also developed and examined hard-to-measure phenomena such as indicators for severity assessments and perception of social networks.

Example:

I have co-authored five research papers on subjects such as weapon holding, child recruitment, and family reintegration in Iraq.

Deliverables

I am committed to reproducible research. Therefore, upon completing the consultancy, you will receive well-documented R scripts, along with R Markdown/Quarto files detailing each step of the work, clean datasets in the required format (Excel, CSV, SPSS, etc.), and/or an analytical report.
